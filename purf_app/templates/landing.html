About PURF

PURF is a web tool designed to help students find the right research advisor for them. It includes data from professors’ personal research pages, past student projects, and student reviews for the Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Chemistry, and Molecular Biology departments.

The site is hosted on Heroku, and was developed using Django and PostgreSQL.

It was created by Jean Choi, Stephen Cognetta, Pallavi Koppol, Eugene Lee, and Qinlan Shen.
